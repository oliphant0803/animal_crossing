Multiplayer

Visiting is when you visit another player's town. It can also be classed as visiting if a villager asks you to come over or if they can come to your house. When you are visiting each other they can fish, do bug-catching, chat with each other, or shop in the stores.

Art Museum

The museum is an establishment for the collection and display of all of the collectables that you have found, including fish, deep-sea creatures, bugs, fossils, paintings, and sculptures. These can be donated only once to the museum curator in the respective exhibit.

Wedding Season

During the event, players who visit Harv's Island have the opportunity to do a special wedding-themed photo shoot featuring Reese and Cyrus. You will be rewarded with wedding-themed items for taking the best wedding photo. You will be able to use as much or as little wedding themed furniture matches the preferred theme.

Firework events

Once a week, you can watch fireworks fly through the night's sky and you can even add your own Custom Fireworks to the display. There is also the Redd Raffle, which sees everyone's favourite kitsune handing out some exciting prizes. From Fireworks Shows in summer to Toy Day on Christmas, immerse in the festive celebrations.
