Who are they?

Types of villiagers in Animal Crossing

  • Snooty and Cranky villagers can come off snobbish, arrogant, and hostile towards other characters and the player, over time, they can warm up, but still appear somewhat arrogant.

  • Sisterly and Smug villagers will come off as considerate and caring towards the player and other villagers, almost as parental-like figures. In New Leaf, they will almost always give the player medicine when they have been stung by a bee.

  • Normal and Lazy villagers appear very calm for the majority, but are also very kind and caring. They will usually get along with the player and other villagers for the most part.

  • Peppy and Jock villagers are typically friendly as well, but can also be somewhat childish and competitive.

cutest villagers on my island

Filbert

Personality: Lazy

Species: Squirrel

As a lazy villager, Filbert will be friendly and easy to get along with due to his laid back lifestyle. Like all lazy villagers, he has a love of food and relaxing. He will enjoy partaking in the usual hobbies, usually for relaxing reasons or for food, like when fishing. Filbert will get along well with other villagers, but may offend or confuse jock villagers, who have a lifestyle of exercise and fitness, which conflicts with the lazy lifestyle of relaxing and food.

Tammy

Personality: Sisterly

Species: Cub

Tammy is a sisterly villager. When befriended, she will give the player fighting tips and teach them ways to relax. Tammy will also give the player medicine if they are stung by a bee. Like all sisterly villagers, Tammy is tough, but she will still care deeply about her appearance. Tammy has a tendency to stay up very late at night. Developing friendships with sisterly villagers is simple due to her caring and protective nature.

Carmen

Personality: Peppy

Species: Rabbit

Peppy villagers appear to be in a good mood often and are easy to become friends with. As a peppy villager, Carmen will have the tendency to over-react in conversations about trivial subjects, and will usually be over-excited to see the player or other villagers. Sje will rarely be discouraged from doing anything.

Dom

Personality: Jock

Species: Sheep

Dom has a jock personality and has a great interest in sports and bodybuilding. Jock villagers are hyperactive and motivated but often come across as egotistical and dense. As a jock villager, Dom is very interested in his hobby, and may compete against the player at catching bugs and fish.

Freya

Personality: Snooty

Species: Wolf

Freya has a snooty personality, which means she loves make-up and gossiping. As a snooty villager, Freya will first appear rude and arrogant towards the player, often talking about herself and her own experiences. She also speaks about the style and appearance of other villagers, usually other female villagers such as normal, peppy, and other snooty villagers.

Julian

Personality: Smug

Species: Horse

Smug villagers are very polite, kind, and gentleman-like. As a smug villager, Julian will get along easily with other villagers. Smug villagers get along well with most villagers, their personality appearing to be a mix of the other personality types. As a smug villager he will stroke his ego from time to time, going on about how cool he is.

Poppy

Personality: Normal

Species: Squirrel

Poppy has a normal personality, and frequently acts kind towards the player. Normal villagers generally get along well with lazy, peppy, smug, snooty and other normal villagers. Poppy may conflict with cranky and sisterly villagers. As a normal villager, Poppy will usually wake up at 6:00 am and is easier to befriend than most.

Biff

Personality: Jock

Species: Hippo

Biff has a jock personality and has a great interest in sports and bodybuilding. Jock villagers are hyperactive and motivated but often come across as egotistical and dense. As a jock villager, Biff is very interested in his hobby, and may compete against the player at catching bugs and fish.
