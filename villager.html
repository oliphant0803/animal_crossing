Who are they?

Types of villiagers in Animal Crossing

  • Snooty and Cranky villagers can come off snobbish, arrogant, and hostile towards other characters and the player, over time, they can warm up, but still appear somewhat arrogant.

  • Sisterly and Smug villagers will come off as considerate and caring towards the player and other villagers, almost as parental-like figures. In New Leaf, they will almost always give the player medicine when they have been stung by a bee.

  • Normal and Lazy villagers appear very calm for the majority, but are also very kind and caring. They will usually get along with the player and other villagers for the most part.

  • Peppy and Jock villagers are typically friendly as well, but can also be somewhat childish and competitive.

cutest villagers on my island

Filbert

Personality: Lazy

Type: Squirrel

Birthday: Jun 3rd

Skills: Vault

Filbert

As a lazy villager, Filbert will be friendly and easy to get along with due to his laid back lifestyle. Like all lazy villagers, he has a love of food and relaxing. He will enjoy partaking in the usual hobbies, usually for relaxing reasons or for food, like when fishing.

Tammy

Personality: Sisterly

Type: Cub

Birthday: Jun 23rd

Skills: Positivity

Tammy

Tammy is a sisterly villager. When befriended, she will give the player fighting tips and teach them ways to relax. Tammy has a tendency to stay up very late at night. Developing friendships with sisterly villagers is simple due to her caring and protective nature.

Carmen

Personality: Peppy

Type: Rabbit

Birthday: Jan 6th

Skills: Illusion

Carmen

Peppy villagers appear to be in a good mood often and are easy to become friends with. As a peppy villager, Carmen will have the tendency to over-react in conversations about trivial subjects, and will usually be over-excited to see the player or other villagers.

Dom

Personality: Jock

Type: Sheep

Birthday: Mar 18th

Skills: Circles

Dom

Dom has a jock personality and has a great interest in sports and bodybuilding. Dom is hyperactive and motivated but often come across as egotistical and dense. Dom is very interested in his hobby, and may compete against the player at catching bugs and fish.

Freya

Personality: Snooty

Type: Wolf

Birthday: Dec 14th

Skills: Peeling

Freya

Freya has a snooty personality, which means she loves make-up and gossiping. Freya will first appear rude and arrogant towards the player, often talking about herself and her own experiences. She also speaks about the style and appearance of normal, peppy, and other snooty villagers.

Julian

Personality: Smug

Type: Horse

Birthday: Mar 15th

Skills: Reader

Julian

Smug villagers are very polite, kind, and gentleman-like. Julian will get along easily with other villagers. Smug villagers get along well with most villagers, their personality appearing to be a mix of the other personality types. He will stroke his ego going on about how cool he is.

Poppy

Personality: Normal

Type: Squirrel

Birthday: Aug 5th

Skills: Budget

Poppy

Poppy has a normal personality, and frequently acts kind towards the player. Normal villagers generally get along well with lazy, peppy, smug, snooty and other normal villagers. Poppy may conflict with cranky and sisterly villagers. As a normal villager.

Biff

Personality: Jock

Species: Hippo

Birthday: Mar 29th

Skills: Limboing

Biff

Biff has a jock personality and has a great interest in sports and bodybuilding. Jock villagers are hyperactive and motivated but often come across as egotistical and dense. Biff is very interested in his hobby, and may compete against the player at catching bugs and fish.
